Here you'll find a selection of my most recent and
meaningful writing projects, including internal

Internal Knowledge Base Articles — Zwift

At Zwift, I created and maintained internal documentation for Community Support agents using MadCap Flare. I collaborated with subject matter experts to fill gaps in the knowledge base and reviewed draft articles for clarity and accuracy. These guides helped streamline onboarding and customer support.

Client-Facing Documentation — Folia Materials

At Folia Materials, I contributed to early-stage technical and marketing documentation. I reviewed investor-facing slide decks for clarity and tone, and collaborated on building external FAQ content. I also researched and wrote content on recyclable susceptors, designed for conventions and trade shows.

“Microsoft Word Manual” — Technical Writing Course Project

This 28-page manual helps students navigate Microsoft Word while drafting and formatting their senior theses. Aimed at users more familiar with Google Docs, it covers everything from page setup and footnotes to revision tools and PDF export. With annotated visuals and clear, step-by-step instructions, the manual focuses on making Word’s features approachable and practical for academic writers.
